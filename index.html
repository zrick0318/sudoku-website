About

About us.

YANHONG is a game studio focused on mobile games. We believe the best games are easy to pick up and hard to put down — whether you have five minutes or an hour.

We’ve released multiple titles with different genres and play styles: puzzle games for logic lovers, casual arcade games for quick sessions, and more. Each game is designed to be clear, fair, and enjoyable without pay-to-win pressure.

Our goal is simple: make games that bring a bit of fun to your day. Explore our games below and find your next favorite.

Products

Our games.

Sudoku King

A sudoku game for everyone

Classic number puzzles with multiple difficulty levels — from beginner to expert. Daily challenges, auto-saved progress, and a clean, ad-light interface so you can focus on the grid. Perfect for commutes or quiet time.

Fishing King

Cartoon-style fishing arcade

Cast your line into colorful waters and reel in fish in this casual arcade game. Charming cartoon visuals, simple one-hand controls, and satisfying catches. Great for short sessions and players of all ages.
